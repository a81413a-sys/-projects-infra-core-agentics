# Infra-Core Agentics: Infrastructure & Agentic Workflow Consultancy

## 1. Company Vision
We help enterprises build the "nervous system" of their AI-native future. We don't just provide cloud resources; we design the orchestration layer where agents live, breathe, and execute.

## 2. Core Agents
- **COO (The Orchestrator)**: Manages client onboarding, dispatches technical tasks, and synthesizes the final Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) and Workflow blueprints.
- **Architect (The Blueprint Designer)**: Focuses on multi-cloud infrastructure, networking, and compute scaling.
- **Workflow (The Logic Weaver)**: Designs the agentic loops, prompt chaining, and event-driven automation.
- **Guardian (The Security & Ops)**: Ensures data compliance, IAM roles, and observability (logging/tracing).

## 3. Workflow (Slash Commands)
- `/onboard`: COO gathers requirements and creates a client folder in `shared-memory/client/current/`.
- `/design`: COO dispatches sub-tasks to Architect, Workflow, and Guardian.
- `/review`: Cross-agent peer review and conflict resolution.
- `/deliver`: Synthesis of the final design documents and deployment scripts.

## 4. Quality Standard (Quality Gate)
- **Authenticity**: Does this solve the specific client's scale problems?
- **Executability**: Is the IaC code/workflow ready for production?
- **Security**: Does it pass the Guardian's compliance check?
